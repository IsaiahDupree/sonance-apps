Privacy Policy

Last updated July 4, 2026. This policy covers all Mac apps by Isaiah Dupree.

All appsContact

The short version

These apps are built to work entirely on your Mac. They do not collect, store, or transmit your personal content, and they have no accounts, logins, analytics, tracking, or advertising.

What the apps access — and where it stays

Each app only touches what it needs to do its job, and that data never leaves your device:

  • Sonance EQ, Mixer, Recorder process audio locally through macOS Core Audio; recordings are written only to the folder you choose.
  • Sonance Voice processes microphone audio locally in real time.
  • Lumen Grab performs text recognition (OCR) on-device using Apple's Vision framework; captured images are not stored or sent anywhere.
  • Lumen Pick reads a pixel color you point at.
  • Clip stores your clipboard history locally on your Mac; you can exclude sensitive sources such as password managers.
  • Menu Vitals reads system statistics and the process list locally.
  • SnapZones uses the macOS Accessibility API to move windows; it reads nothing about their contents.
  • DevForge transforms text you enter entirely offline.

Purchases

Some apps offer an optional one-time “Pro” purchase. Purchases are handled by Apple through the App Store. To validate and remember your purchase across your devices, we use RevenueCat, a purchase-management service that receives an anonymous identifier and basic device information — never your name, email, or the content you use the app with. We do not receive your payment details; Apple handles all payment information. See also Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data we collect

None. We do not operate servers that receive your content, and we do not use analytics or tracking SDKs. Apps that are still in evaluation run their purchase flow locally with no network activity at all.

Permissions you may be asked for

  • Audio capture (Sonance EQ / Mixer / Recorder) and microphone (Sonance Voice) — to process the audio you ask the app to handle.
  • Screen Recording (Lumen Grab) — required by macOS to read the pixels you select for OCR.
  • Accessibility (SnapZones) — required by macOS to move and resize windows.

These permissions are granted by you in System Settings and can be revoked at any time.

Children

These apps are not directed at children and do not knowingly collect any information from anyone.
