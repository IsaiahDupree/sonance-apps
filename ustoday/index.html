Us Today for iPhone

Small steps, closer together.

A calm 7-Day Reset for your relationship. Check in for 60 seconds, get one thoughtful thing to do today, and see gentle progress — no scores, no judgment.

About

Us Today helps you show up for your relationship one small, doable step at a time. Each day you do a quick check-in about how things feel, and the app suggests a single personalized action — with the exact words to say if you want them. You mark what you did and how it landed, and over the week you see your own patterns.

HOW IT WORKS
• A 60-second daily check-in (connection, tension, energy, time)
• One approved, personalized action for today — never a generic tip
• Suggested wording you can make your own
• Gentle weekly progress — patterns, not a grade

Private by design: your check-ins stay on your device. No account required, no ads, no tracking.

Getting started

  1. Open Us Today and start your 7-Day Reset.
  2. Do the 60-second check-in — just how things feel, no wrong answers.
  3. Try today's one action; mark it done and say how it landed.
  4. Come back tomorrow — the app learns what helps you.

A note on safety

Us Today is for everyday relationship care — it is not crisis support or a tool for unsafe situations. If a check-in mentions self-harm or fear for your safety, the app steps back from exercises and points to real help. If you are in immediate danger, contact your local emergency number. In the US & Canada you can call or text 988; the National Domestic Violence Hotline is 1-800-799-7233.
