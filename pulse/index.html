Getting started

  1. Open the menu-bar panel to see live CPU, memory, network, and disk.
  2. Scroll to Top Processes to see what's busy.
  3. Click the ✕ next to a process to Quit or Force Quit it — free.

Frequently asked questions

How do I stop a process?

Open the panel, find the process in Top Processes, and choose Quit or Force Quit. This is free.

What's the Pro feature?

Choosing which stat (CPU, memory, network, battery) rides in your menu bar. The full panel and stopping processes are free.

Does it slow my Mac down?

No — it samples lightweightly and stays out of the way.
