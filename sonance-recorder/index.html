About

Record the audio your Mac is playing — the whole system or a single app — straight to a clean file. No cables, no loopback driver, no “Stereo Mix” hacks.

GREAT FOR
• Saving a podcast, interview, or webinar as it plays
• Capturing a video call for notes (with everyone's consent)
• Grabbing audio from a stream, game, or app that has no download
• Ripping just one app's sound while everything else keeps playing

Your audio keeps playing normally through your speakers while Sonance Recorder writes a pristine copy in the background. Built on Core Audio process taps (macOS 14.4+) — driverless and reboot-free.

FEATURES
• Record the whole system to lossless WAV (free)
• Record a single chosen app in isolation (Pro)
• Space-saving Apple Lossless & AAC formats (Pro)
• One-click record from the menu bar + reveal in Finder

Free: whole-system WAV. Pro (one-time purchase): per-app capture + compact formats.

Everything is processed on your Mac and never leaves it.

Getting started

  1. Open the menu-bar icon and choose a source (whole system or one app).
  2. Click record — your audio keeps playing normally.
  3. Click stop, then Reveal in Finder to find your file.

Frequently asked questions

Where are my recordings saved?

To the folder you choose; use “Reveal in Finder” from the menu bar after a recording.

Can I record just one app?

Yes — single-app capture is a Pro feature. Free records the whole system to lossless WAV.

Does audio keep playing while I record?

Yes. Your audio plays normally while a clean copy is written in the background.
