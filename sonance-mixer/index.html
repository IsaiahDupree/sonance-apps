About

macOS still has no built-in per-app volume control. Sonance Mixer adds it — a menu-bar mixer with a separate volume slider and mute for every app currently playing sound.

GREAT FOR
• Turning a loud game down without touching your Discord call
• Keeping background music quiet while you're on Zoom
• Instantly muting one noisy browser tab or app — not everything
• Sending a movie to your speakers while notifications stay on your headphones (Pro)

No driver, no kernel extension, no reboot. Sonance Mixer uses Core Audio process taps (macOS 14.4+), so it installs like any normal app and adjusts every level live, with no clicks or glitches.

FEATURES
• Independent volume + mute for every app playing audio
• Per-app output routing (Pro) — send different apps to different devices
• Driverless and lightweight — lives quietly in your menu bar
• Smooth, real-time changes with no zipper noise

Free: volume + mute for every app. Pro (one-time purchase): per-app output routing.

Your audio is processed live on your Mac and never leaves it.

Getting started

  1. Click the menu-bar icon to open the mixer.
  2. Play audio in any app — it appears with its own slider.
  3. Drag a slider to set that app's volume, or click the speaker to mute.

Frequently asked questions

An app isn't showing in the list.

Only apps currently playing audio appear. Start playback and it will show up with its own slider.

Can I send different apps to different speakers?

Yes — per-app output routing is a Pro feature. Free includes independent volume and mute for every app.

Do I need a driver?

No. It's driverless via Core Audio process taps (macOS 14.4+).
