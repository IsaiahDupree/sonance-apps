About

Make your microphone sound like a studio — in real time, in any app. Sonance Voice cleans up and warms up your voice before it ever reaches Zoom, Meet, Discord, or your recorder.

GREAT FOR
• Sounding clear and professional on video calls
• Recording voiceovers and podcasts without a pricey interface
• Cutting keyboard clatter, fans, and room hum out of your mic
• Adding radio-style warmth and presence to a thin-sounding mic

A full processing chain — high-pass, noise gate, warmth, presence, and compression — runs live with low latency. Monitor it on your headphones, then record the enhanced result. Built on Apple's own audio engine.

FEATURES
• Real-time enhancement: noise reduction, warmth, presence, compression
• Live level meter + zero-latency headphone monitoring (free)
• Record the enhanced voice straight to a file (Pro)
• Simple, friendly menu-bar controls

Free: live enhancement + monitoring. Pro (one-time purchase): recording.

Processed entirely on your Mac.

Getting started

  1. Open the app and pick your microphone.
  2. Toggle the enhancements you want (noise reduction, warmth, compression).
  3. Select “Sonance Voice” as the mic in Zoom, Meet, or your recorder.
