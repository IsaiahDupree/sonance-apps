About

Tile your windows instantly. Click a layout or press a keyboard shortcut and the focused window snaps to a half, quarter, or third of the screen — a clean, fast, native window manager in your menu bar.

GREAT FOR
• Two apps side by side without dragging and resizing
• A tidy three-column layout for research or coding
• Instantly maximizing or centering the front window
• Arranging your desktop entirely from the keyboard

FEATURES
• Left/right/top/bottom halves, maximize, and center (free)
• Control-Option + arrow-key shortcuts
• Quarters and thirds for dense side-by-side layouts (Pro)
• A visual layout picker in the menu bar

Free: halves + maximize + center. Pro (one-time purchase): quarters + thirds.

Uses the macOS Accessibility API to move windows — nothing leaves your Mac.

Getting started

  1. Grant Accessibility permission so Snap can move windows.
  2. Click a layout in the menu-bar panel, or use Control-Option + arrow keys.
  3. The focused window snaps into place instantly.

Frequently asked questions

It won't move my windows.

SnapZones needs Accessibility permission to move other apps' windows. Grant it in System Settings ▸ Privacy & Security ▸ Accessibility.

What are the keyboard shortcuts?

Control-Option + arrow keys snap the focused window to halves, plus maximize and center.

What's Pro?

Quarters and thirds. Halves, maximize, and center are free.
