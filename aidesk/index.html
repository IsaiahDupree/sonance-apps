About

AI Desk is a native macOS chat workspace for AI models. You bring your own API keys; AI Desk gives you a fast, keyboard-friendly desk to work at.

FEATURES
• Multi-chat workspace with streaming responses
• Works with your own API keys — xAI (Grok), OpenAI, Anthropic, Google
• Voice input with live transcription (⌥Space)
• Chat about your files — drop in documents, code, or images
• Workspace memory and reusable assistant presets
• Menu-bar quick assistant, full markdown rendering

Your keys stay in the macOS Keychain on your Mac. Conversations go directly from your Mac to the provider you choose — no middleman servers, no account, no tracking.

Getting started

  1. Open Settings (⌘,) and paste an API key from your AI provider.
  2. Start a chat — responses stream in live.
  3. Drop in files to discuss them; press ⌥Space for voice input.

Frequently asked questions

Where do I get an API key?

From your provider's console — e.g. console.x.ai, platform.openai.com, console.anthropic.com, or aistudio.google.com. Paste it into AI Desk's Settings.

Is my data private?

Yes. AI Desk has no servers and no analytics. Keys live in your macOS Keychain; chats go directly to the provider you configured and are stored locally on your Mac.

Why do responses cost money?

You pay your AI provider directly for API usage under your own account. AI Desk itself is free.
