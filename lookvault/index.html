About

LookVault turns outfit inspiration into something you can actually wear. Open a look and LookVault scores how closely you can recreate it from your own closet, breaking every piece down as Owned, Similar, or Missing — with three ways to pull it off.

THREE WAYS TO RECREATE ANY LOOK
• Exact — shop the real pieces
• Use what I own — style it from your closet
• Budget — smart, affordable alternatives

Plus a Discover feed ranked by what's trending and achievable, a Wardrobe with cost-per-wear analytics, Vault collections, a weather-aware Outfit of the Day, and an Ask Stylist assistant grounded in your real wardrobe.

Private by design: LookVault works offline in Demo Mode with no account. The virtual try-on is a style visualization, not a fit guarantee.

Getting started

  1. Open LookVault — it starts with a set of sample looks and a sample wardrobe.
  2. Tap any look to see its Recreation Score and piece-by-piece breakdown.
  3. Switch between Exact, Use what I own, and Budget to see your options.
  4. Explore your Wardrobe, save looks to your Vault, and check Today's outfit.

Frequently asked questions

Do I need an account?

No. LookVault works entirely on your device in Demo Mode with no sign-up.

Are the outfit images real photos?

In this version, garment images are stylized placeholder swatches. The Recreation Score, breakdown, and analytics are all real, computed from the sample data.

Is the try-on a guarantee of fit?

No — the virtual try-on is a style visualization, not a fit guarantee.

Is it free?

Yes — LookVault is free.
