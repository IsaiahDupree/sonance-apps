Family Pulse for iPhone & iPad

How's everyone, really?

A calmer family check-in. Everyone shares how their day went in under a minute, and Family Pulse turns it into one warm daily brief — plus a huddle to celebrate, solve, and reconnect.

About

Family Pulse helps a household stay close without another noisy feed. Each person does a quick check-in — mood, energy, the one thing about their day, anything they need help with, and something they appreciated. The app gathers it into a single calm brief: who had a good day, who could use a little care, what to celebrate, and one gentle nudge for the family today.

HOW IT WORKS
• A under-a-minute daily check-in (with a simpler version for young kids)
• One warm family brief — Today, Needs care, Help, Celebrate, and a single nudge
• A family huddle: Celebrate / Solve / Connect cards, and Solve items become shared promises
• A weekly reset that prepares your Sunday sit-down for you

Private by design: in Demo Mode everything stays on your device. No account required, no ads, no tracking. Kids don't need an email.

Getting started

  1. Open Family Pulse — it starts with a sample family so you can explore right away.
  2. Add your own family members (parents, teens, kids) with an avatar and color.
  3. Everyone does the quick daily check-in.
  4. Read the family brief, and run a huddle when you want to talk things through.

An honest note on the "brief"

The family brief is generated on your device by simple, transparent rules — it reflects only what people choose to share. It is not a diagnosis and never assesses anyone's mental health. Family Pulse is for everyday connection, not crisis support. If someone is in danger, contact your local emergency number; in the US & Canada you can call or text 988.

Frequently asked questions

Do we need accounts?

No. Family Pulse works entirely on your device in Demo Mode with no sign-up. Optional Live Mode (to sync across phones) uses your own private Supabase project.

Do my kids need an email?

No. Children are added as managed profiles by a parent — no separate login.

Is it free?

Yes — the full app is free.

Is our data private?

Yes. No account, no analytics, no tracking. Your check-ins stay on your device unless you choose to connect your own cloud.
