About

Your clipboard, with a memory. Everything you copy is kept, searchable, and one hotkey away — so you never lose something you copied ten minutes (or ten copies) ago.

GREAT FOR
• Getting back text you copied over by accident
• Pasting from several things you copied earlier, in any order
• Keeping snippets, links, and addresses you reuse all day
• Searching your copy history instead of hunting for it again

Lightweight, native, and private: your history stays on your Mac, and you can keep password managers out of it automatically.

FEATURES
• Automatic clipboard history — text, links, and images
• Instant search across everything you've copied
• Your last 20 items (free)
• Unlimited history + pinned favorites (Pro)

Free: last 20 + search. Pro (one-time purchase): unlimited history + pins.

Stays entirely on your device.

Frequently asked questions

How do I open my history?

Press the hotkey (⌘⇧V) or click the menu-bar icon, then click any item to copy it again.

How much history is kept?

The last 20 items are free; unlimited history and pinned favorites are Pro.

Is my clipboard private?

Yes — history stays on your Mac. You can exclude password managers automatically.
